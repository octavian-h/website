DexOffline

October 21, 2020 · Octavian Hasna

Overview

These applications help you find words in DEX (the Romanian language dictionary) without being online. Search words can be saved in history. Besides, you can check if a word is in the official Scrabble word list for Romanian language, you can read the feed with words of the day, generate valid words for the Scrabble game, play word games and much more.
